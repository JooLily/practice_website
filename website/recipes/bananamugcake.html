Banana Mug Cake

Back to Recipes Home

This recipe is from Molly's Banana Mug Cake on vanillabeancuisine.com

Description

This recipe is a perfect single-serving banana bread that's quick and simple to make with only a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of an overripe banana
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • (optional)1 tablespoon chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pick a mug that is not too tall or too thick. The mug needs to be microwave-safe.
  2. Cut the overripe banana in half and peel it. Mash the banana into the mug.
  3. Measure out the wet ingredients (oil and milk) into the mug. Mix together.
  4. Measure out the flour into a measuring cup. I like to do half flour and half protein powder.
  5. Measure out the remaining dry ingredients into the measuring cup with the flour.
    • The remaining dry ingredients are brown sugar, cinnamon, salt, and baking powder.
    • I substitute baking powder with a little bit of lemon juice. I don't know if it works as a substitute on its own, though.
  6. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients in the mug, and add in your preferred topping. I prefer oats instead of chocolate chips.
  7. Scrape down the sides of the mug for even baking.
  8. Microwave on high for 80-90 seconds.
  9. Let cool for about a minute and then enjoy!
